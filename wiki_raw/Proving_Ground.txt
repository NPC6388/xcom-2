The '''Proving Ground''' is a {{IconHeader|Facility}} aboard the [[Avenger (XCOM 2)|Avenger]] in [[XCOM 2|''XCOM 2'']].
{{Quote|An experimental work space where Shen can test and produce new, unique types of ammo, grenades and armor for our soldiers, as well as other specialized tactical items.|In-game description}}

==Functionality==
<section begin="Functionality" />
* Allows for the creation of experimental equipment and specialized suits of armor for soldiers.
<u>'''Staffing:'''</u>
<br />{{IconHeader|Engineer}} - Decrease Proving Ground Project Time by 50%.<section end="Functionality" />
