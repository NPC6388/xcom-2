# Early × Upgrades

**Phase:** Early (Gatecrasher → first Skulljack) | **Section:** Upgrades | **Leads with:** Timing/tempo
**Difficulty:** Commander | **Scope:** base + integrated DLC, no WOTC
Covers: GTS squad-size upgrades, weapon mods, Proving Ground experimental items, first gear-tier builds, ability picks. (SPARK/Shen's Last Gift mostly lands mid-game → see [mid-upgrades](mid-upgrades.md).)

> Tempo thesis: the highest-value early "upgrade" isn't gear — it's the **5th squad slot**.
> After that, cheap **weapon mods** and a **Proving Ground experimental grenade** give the
> best power-per-resource before you can afford full Mag/Plated tiers.

## Ability picks (early ranks) — from your Steam ability thread
Use these when leveling the A-team; they bias toward early tempo/scouting:
- **Ranger:** Phantom → Shadowstrike / Conceal (scouting); Bladestorm situational.
- **Sniper:** Long Watch, Lightning Hands, Quick Draw early; **Serial** is a standout later.
- **Grenadier:** **Shred** (mandatory cover removal), Demolition, Holotargeting.
- **Specialist #1 — HACKER (offensive Gremlin tree):** **Combat Protocol** (guaranteed Gremlin damage, esp. vs robotic) → **Haywire Protocol** (hack/control turrets, MECs, later Sectopods) → **Capacitor Discharge** (AoE) → Guardian. Prioritize raising the **Hacking stat** (see below) so Haywire reliably lands on tough robotic targets.
- **Specialist #2 — MEDIC (support tree):** **Medical Protocol** → **Revival Protocol** → **Field Medic** (esp. before AWC online) → **Restoration** (AoE heal/revive). This is your sustain backbone.

> Run these as two distinct Specialists, not one hybrid — the hacker leans into Hacking-stat gear, the medic into healing throughput.

### Raising the hacker's Hacking stat (so Haywire works on hard targets)
- **Skullmining**: skulljack hacks that succeed grant permanent **+Hacking / +Intel** — feed the hacker the skulljack.
- **PCS implants**: equip a **Hacking PCS** (Personal Combat Sim) when available; higher tiers give bigger boosts.
- **Battlefield hacks**: successfully hacking objectives/workstations can roll Hacking bonuses.
- Source for methods: [StackExchange: raise hacking](https://gaming.stackexchange.com/questions/254638/how-can-i-raise-a-soldiers-hacking-ability) *(couldn't auto-fetch; reconstructed — verify in-game)*.
