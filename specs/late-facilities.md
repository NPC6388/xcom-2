# Late × Facilities

**Phase:** Late (Avatar facilities → final mission) | **Section:** Facilities | **Leads with:** Timing/tempo
**Difficulty:** Commander | **Scope:** base + integrated DLC, no WOTC

> Tempo thesis: the Avenger is mostly built — late game is **optimization and staffing**, not new
> construction. Make sure every facility is fully staffed for the final push and you can survive an
> **Avenger Defense** mission if it comes.

## Build / optimization table

| # | Facility action | Priority | Why now (tempo) | Trap to avoid | Source |
|---|-----------------|----------|-----------------|---------------|--------|
| 1 | **Full staffing pass** (engineers/scientists in every slot) | **Critical** | Staffed Shadow Chamber/Psi Lab/AWC/Comms = faster research, training, heals right when it matters most | Leaving facilities half-staffed and bottlenecking the finale prep | [UFOpaedia: Avenger Opt.](https://www.ufopaedia.org/index.php/Avenger_Optimization_(XCOM2)) |
| 2 | **Power headroom / redundant relay** | High | Final-tier production + everything staffed draws max power; a buffer prevents a last-minute stall | Running at exactly 0 surplus and getting blocked when you add the last upgrade | [UFOpaedia: Avenger Opt.](https://www.ufopaedia.org/index.php/Avenger_Optimization_(XCOM2)) |
| 3 | **Finish/staff Psi Lab** (if psi path) | High *if* psi | Mature a 2nd Psi Operative or finish ability unlocks before the finale | Leaving a half-trained Psi Op who never reaches the strong abilities | [tech-tree](tech-tree.md) |
| 4 | **Avenger Defense readiness** | Medium | A late Avatar facility assault or story beat can trigger a base-defense fight — know your turret/soldier positions | Being caught with your A-team wounded and no plan | [Fandom: Avatar](https://xcom.fandom.com/wiki/Avatar_Project) |

## Notes
- **No new core facilities** are typically needed late — if you skipped something earlier (e.g. Psi Lab) it's likely too late to mature it; don't sink resources into a dead-end build.
- **Power coils:** by now every Exposed Power Coil should host a relay or a high-draw facility.
- Keep **AWC** staffed — fast heals keep your finale roster intact across the back-to-back late missions.

### Scope flags
- Vanilla base layout only; no WOTC facilities.
