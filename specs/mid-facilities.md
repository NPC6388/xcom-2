# Mid × Facilities

**Phase:** Mid (first Skulljack → Avatar Project reveal) | **Section:** Facilities | **Leads with:** Timing/tempo
**Difficulty:** Commander | **Scope:** base + integrated DLC, no WOTC

> Tempo thesis: mid game adds the **Shadow Chamber** (mandatory for the Avatar chain + it reveals
> enemy squads on the mission screen) and, if you're going psi, the **Psi Lab**. Both are
> power-hungry — keep using **Exposed Power Coils** and stay ahead of power, or your timeline stalls.

## Notes
- **Power is the recurring constraint** — reserve Exposed Power Coils for the Shadow Chamber and Psi Lab (highest draw), not cheap rooms.
- **One of each is enough** mid game; don't duplicate beyond Comms/Power.
- **AWC** (built early) keeps paying off here via heals + bonus abilities as your roster deepens.

### Scope flags
- No **Resistance Ring / Training Center** (WOTC). Psi Lab + AWC + Shadow Chamber are the vanilla mid-game additions.
