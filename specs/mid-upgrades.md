# Mid × Upgrades

**Phase:** Mid (first Skulljack → Avatar Project reveal) | **Section:** Upgrades | **Leads with:** Timing/tempo
**Difficulty:** Commander | **Scope:** base + integrated DLC, no WOTC
Covers: full mag/Gauss tier, Predator→toward Warden, Proving Ground experimental armor/ammo, SPARK (Shen's Last Gift), Psi Operatives, PCS implants.

> Tempo thesis: get the **whole squad off conventional gear** (mag rifle + Gauss cannon/sniper),
> bank an **experimental armor** for your scouts, and bring **Bluescreen rounds** online for the
> robotic enemies (Codex/MEC/Sectopod) that define mid game. Decide your **SPARK** and **Psi**
> investments now — both pay off but need lead time.

## Notes
- **Skullmining:** keep feeding skulljack hacks to **Specialist #1** to grow Hacking (and bank the Intel). See [early-upgrades](early-upgrades.md).
- **Armor transition:** Predator everyone, then start banking alloys/cores toward Powered/Warden once Elerium lands (late).
- **Grenadier:** consider an experimental/plasma grenade upgrade as it becomes available — AoE keeps clearing scaled pods.

### Scope flags
- SPARK is **Shen's Last Gift** (in scope). No WOTC SPARK perks/Lost-tower variants.
