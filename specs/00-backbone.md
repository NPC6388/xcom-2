## EARLY GAME — Gatecrasher → first Skulljack / first blacksite
**Tempo goal:** Be **6 soldiers strong in Magnetic Weapons + Predator Armor** *before* you Skulljack an ADVENT Officer. Everything early is in service of hitting that bar on time.

| Beat | Trigger | Research → | Build → | Upgrade → |
|------|---------|-----------|---------|-----------|
| 1 · Foundation | Post-Gatecrasher | [Alien Biotech → Modular Weapons](early-research.md) | [Rush **GTS** with 1st engineer](early-facilities.md) | — (excavate/contact) |
| 2 · Reach | 1–2 regions / 2nd scientist | [Resistance Comms → **Magnetic Weapons**](early-research.md#order-table) | [**Power Relay** on coil; build Resistance Comms](early-facilities.md) | [**Squad Size I** (5th soldier); weapon mods](early-upgrades.md) |
| 3 · Power spike | Alloys/cores in hand | [Plated Armor → **ADVENT Officer Autopsy**](early-research.md) | [**Proving Ground** + AWC](early-facilities.md) | [Exp. grenade; **Squad Size II**; build Mag/Predator; **Skulljack**](early-upgrades.md) |

➡️ **Transition to MID when:** you build the **Skulljack** and skulljack an ADVENT Officer — this spawns a **Codex** and reveals the path toward the Avatar Project. Don't cross this line under-geared (see early-upgrades row 6). *(Verify Codex-spawn detail vs. skulljack StackExchange.)*

## MID GAME — Skulljack → Codex → Avatar reveal
**Tempo goal:** Win the **race to Elerium** (needs Plated Armor + Gauss) while starting the **Shadow Chamber / Codex Brain** chain — get the whole squad onto mag/Gauss + Bluescreen before robot-heavy pods scale.

| Beat | Trigger | Research → | Build → | Upgrade → |
|------|---------|-----------|---------|-----------|
| 1 · Codex chain | Skulljacked Officer → Codex appeared | [Codex Brain; Gauss Weapons](mid-research.md) | [**Shadow Chamber**; Power Relay #2](mid-facilities.md) | [Full mag/Gauss tier; **Bluescreen Rounds**](mid-upgrades.md) |
| 2 · Elerium race | Plated Armor + Mag Cannon done | [**Elerium**; key autopsies](mid-research.md#order-table) | [Resistance Comms #2](mid-facilities.md) | [Experimental Armor; **SPARK** (Lost Towers)](mid-upgrades.md) |
| 3 · Psi fork (opt) | Sectoid Autopsy done | [Psionics](mid-research.md) | [**Psi Lab**](mid-facilities.md) | [Train Psi Op; **Hacking PCS** on Specialist #1](mid-upgrades.md) |

➡️ **Transition to LATE when:** **Elerium** is researched (top tier now unlocked) and the **Avatar Project** is revealed via the Shadow Chamber chain. You're now converting Elerium into plasma/Warden and driving toward the Avatar Autopsy.

## LATE GAME — Avatar facilities → final mission
**Tempo goal:** Convert Elerium into the **top tier (Plasma + Warden)** and drive the **Shadow chain to the Avatar Autopsy** — then trigger the finale only once the A-team is fully equipped.

| Beat | Trigger | Research → | Build → | Upgrade → |
|------|---------|-----------|---------|-----------|
| 1 · Top tier | Elerium done | [**Plasma Rifle**; **Powered Armor**](late-research.md) | [Full staffing; power headroom](late-facilities.md) | [Equip Plasma + **Warden Armor**; Bluescreen stock](late-upgrades.md) |
| 2 · Shadow chain | Each facility assaulted | [Blacksite Vial → Forge → Psi Gate](late-research.md) | [Avenger Defense readiness](late-facilities.md) | [W.A.R./Wraith Suit; **max SPARK / Psi Op**](late-upgrades.md) |
| 3 · Finale prep | All Shadow Projects done | [**Avatar Autopsy** (unlocks finale)](late-research.md) | [Final staffing check](late-facilities.md) | [**Final-mission loadout**: hacker+medic Specialists, Bluescreen, Mimic Beacons](late-upgrades.md) |

➡️ **The finish line:** completing the **Avatar Autopsy** unlocks the final mission. Don't research it until gear + soldiers are finale-ready — you control the timing.
