# Late × Upgrades

**Phase:** Late (Avatar facilities → final mission) | **Section:** Upgrades | **Leads with:** Timing/tempo
**Difficulty:** Commander | **Scope:** base + integrated DLC, no WOTC
Covers: plasma weapons, Warden/powered armor, top Proving Ground gear, maxed SPARK, maxed Psi Ops, final-mission loadout.

> Tempo thesis: late game is **finishing**, not starting. Equip the top tier on the **whole A-team**,
> roll the best Proving Ground suits/ammo, and build a **final-mission loadout** tuned for the
> robotic/psionic enemies of the endgame. Don't trigger the Avatar Autopsy until this is done.

## Notes
- **Hacker finale role:** with a maxed Hacking stat + **Haywire Protocol**, Specialist #1 can seize enemy MECs/Sectopods/turrets in the finale — one of the strongest tempo swings available. Keep a Hacking PCS on them.
- **Medic finale role:** Specialist #2 with **Restoration** keeps the squad topped through the long final fights.
- **What to skip:** flavor autopsies, duplicate suits, any half-finished path you can't mature — finishing the Shadow chain + top tier beats breadth.

### Scope flags
- SPARK = Shen's Last Gift (in scope). No WOTC perks. No Alien Hunters boss-weapons assumed unless you ran that DLC content (Bolt Caster/Hunter's Axe are situational if acquired).
