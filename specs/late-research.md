> then drive the **Shadow Chamber chain to the Avatar Autopsy** to unlock the finale. Research only

## Notes
- **Sequencing:** rows 1–2 (top tier) can run in parallel-ish with the Shadow chain (row 4) — use your scientists to keep both moving; don't let either stall.
- **Finale gating is deliberate:** you control *when* you research the Avatar Autopsy. Hold it until gear + soldiers are finale-ready, then commit.

### Locked ✓ (from parsed tech tree)
- **Avatar Autopsy ← Neutralize Avatar + Psionic Gate + Recovered Stasis Suit** → Broadcast Tower → Final Mission.
- The three feeders: **Neutralize Avatar** (Codex Brain → Encrypted Codex Data → Skulljack Codex → Neutralize Avatar); **Psionic Gate** (Codex Brain Coordinates → Psionic Gate); **Recovered Stasis Suit** (Blacksite Vial → coords → Stasis Suit → Recovered Stasis Suit).
