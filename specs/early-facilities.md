# Early × Facilities

**Phase:** Early (Gatecrasher → first Skulljack) | **Section:** Facilities | **Leads with:** Timing/tempo
**Difficulty:** Commander | **Scope:** base + integrated DLC, no WOTC

> Tempo thesis: **GTS first is non-negotiable** — squad size is your biggest power spike.
> After that, **power is the real gate** on every further build, so manage power coils
> deliberately. Excavate only when you need the slot.

## Notes
- **Power discipline:** save Exposed Power Coils for high-draw facilities (relay here; later Psi Lab/Shadow Chamber) rather than wasting them on cheap rooms.
- **Excavation:** only dig when you need the slot or to reach a coil — don't pre-excavate the whole Avenger early.

### ⚠️ Traps (strong consensus across sources)
- **Never build a Workshop** in vanilla: power cost ties up an engineer, heavy monthly supply upkeep, and the Gremlin-adjacency benefit is outweighed by opportunity cost.
- **Don't build a Laboratory early** — low value vs. the cost; a 2nd scientist from a region beats it.
- **Don't over-excavate** — wasted engineer-time and supplies you need for GTS/Proving Ground.

### Scope flags
- Sources mentioning **Resistance Ring / Training Center** are **WOTC** — those facilities don't exist in vanilla; AWC (row 4) is the vanilla equivalent for the heal/perk role. Filtered out.
